Riot Games announced rating Valorant

by DenZir

Riot Games will launch a rating mode in Valorant from June 22 to 28. This was announced by director of the shooter Joe Ziegler on Twitter. According to him, the next week the studio will spend on preparing this update. In the beta version of the shooter from Riot Games, there were eight available ranks: from Iron to Valorant. Whether the rating system will change after the release is unknown. Previously, the developers promised to rename the highest rank in the game to avoid confusion. The release of Valorant took place on June 2. With the launch of the full version of the game, Riot Games added a combat pass to the shooter, as well as a new map and agent.
